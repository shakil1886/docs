## Managing Your Workflows

Once you have created workflows, you can manage them directly from the <b>Workflow Page</b>.

### Selecting & Executing Multiple Workflows

Select multiple workflows by checking the boxes next to them.

<img
  src="/images/workflow-page.png"
  className="w-full rounded-xl shadow-xl"
/>

Click "RUN WORKFLOW" to run all selected workflows at once.

### Updating & Deleting a Workflow
<ul>
<li>
Click on the workflow you want to edit.
</li>
<li>
Modify test steps and click any where in the app.
</li>
<li>
Click the "Delete" button to remove them permanently.
</li>
</ul>
